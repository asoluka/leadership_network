Annual Conference

Launch Pastors & Leaders Network is a registered platform and organization aimed at creating an atmosphere where every one called into a leadership position can have access to indepth Learning. We build a network-based Assistance, with the aim of Upholding every member, Networking to foster creative relationships, while Challenging them to establish systems and mechanisms to help achieve their ministry goals. OUR VISION Helping gospel ministers and ministries attain full potentials OUR MISSION Learning. Assisting. Upholding. Networking. Challenging. Helping. (The acronym L.A.U.N.C.H.).

Rethink Church conference is a free event which will empower leaders all around the world. It takes place March 19-20, 2020 from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. each day.
